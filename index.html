Welcome to CIM

Chamber of Industries Morang

Established in 1988, CIM is the leading business membership organization in Province No. 1, representing private sector interests and driving industrial development.

With over 500 member industries, we provide a unified voice for the private sector, advocating for policies that foster economic growth, job creation, and sustainable development. Our platform connects businesses, facilitates investment, and promotes Nepal's industrial potential on a global stage.

About Us

Empowering Industries
for a Progressive Nepal

CIM implements programs ranging from issue-based policy advocacy to business development and investment promotion. We are affiliated with FNCC as the apex body of the Nepalese private sector.

Our commitment to excellence has made us the trusted voice of industry, working tirelessly to create an enabling environment for business growth and economic prosperity.

Message from the President

Committed to Industrial Progress

“CIM is dedicated to demystify progressive Nepal through the promotion and development of economic and industrial sectors. We work tirelessly to support our members and advocate for a thriving business environment.”

With over three decades of service, CIM remains the trusted voice of industry. Our focus on policy advocacy, business development, and investment facilitation has helped shape the industrial landscape of Province No. 1.

We invite all stakeholders to join us in our mission to build a progressive and prosperous Nepal through industrial growth and innovation.
